About Daniel

Born on 9th January 1959, my works are based on nature, body and colorful shades. I was born in Morocco where I spent all my childhood in a little neigbourhood of Casablanca. I moved to Paris in 1978 where I received a Bachelor of Arts from Sorbonne University. I also earned a diploma in drawing and painting from the Paris Design School in 1983. Then, I move to Israel and worked as a hairdresser for top models in prestigious magazines while pursuing my passion for painting. My paintings were a part of the contemporary art exhibition held in Tel Aviv in the year 2009.
